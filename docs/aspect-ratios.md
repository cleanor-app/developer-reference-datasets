# What is 16:9 in pixels? Aspect ratios and resolutions, as a table

**16:9 is 1920×1080, 1280×720, 2560×1440, and 3840×2160 — any width times 9/16 gives the height.** The full lookup for 16 common ratios across 11 widths is [`data/aspect-ratios.csv`](../data/aspect-ratios.csv); named resolutions and their reduced ratios are in [`data/resolutions.csv`](../data/resolutions.csv).

## The common ratios

| Ratio | Decimal | Orientation | Where you see it | 1080-wide → | 1920-wide → |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1:1 | 1.000 | square | Instagram post, avatars, icons | 1080×1080 | 1920×1920 |
| 4:3 | 1.333 | landscape | classic TV, iPad, most cameras | 1080×810 | 1920×1440 |
| 3:2 | 1.500 | landscape | 35mm, DSLR sensors | 1080×720 | 1920×1280 |
| 16:9 | 1.778 | landscape | HD/4K video, YouTube | 1080×608 | 1920×1080 |
| 16:10 | 1.600 | landscape | laptop/monitor panels | 1080×675 | 1920×1200 |
| 21:9 | 2.333 | landscape | ultrawide monitors | 1080×463 | 1920×823 |
| 9:16 | 0.563 | portrait | Stories, Reels, TikTok | 1080×1920 | — |
| 4:5 | 0.800 | portrait | Instagram portrait | 1080×1350 | — |
| 2.39:1 | 2.390 | landscape | anamorphic cinema scope | 1080×452 | 1920×803 |

(Heights rounded to the nearest pixel. The CSV carries all 16 ratios at every width from 320 to 3840.)

## Named resolutions

Straight from [`data/resolutions.csv`](../data/resolutions.csv) — the pixel size and the reduced aspect ratio for each:

| Name | Pixels | MP | Ratio |
|---|---|---|---|
| HD (720p) | 1280×720 | 0.92 | 16:9 |
| Full HD (1080p) | 1920×1080 | 2.07 | 16:9 |
| QHD (1440p) | 2560×1440 | 3.69 | 16:9 |
| 4K UHD | 3840×2160 | 8.29 | 16:9 |
| 8K UHD | 7680×4320 | 33.18 | 16:9 |
| UWQHD (ultrawide) | 3440×1440 | 4.95 | 43:18 |
| OG image | 1200×630 | 0.76 | 40:21 |
| Story/Reel | 1080×1920 | 2.07 | 9:16 |

Note the social sizes are not clean 16:9 — an OG image is 40:21 and a Story is 9:16, which is why designs snapped to 16:9 crop wrong on them.

## Method

Pure arithmetic: `height = round(width × ratio_h / ratio_w)`, and ratios reduced with the greatest common divisor. Generated by [`generators/aspect-ratios.mjs`](../generators/aspect-ratios.mjs), no inputs. Re-run to add ratios or widths.
